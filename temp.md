# Tuition Overview

Most German public universities charge **no tuition fees**, requiring only semester contributions.

Typical semester contributions range between:

**€80–€380**

Exceptions include:

- University of Freiburg
- Technical University of Munich (new non-EU tuition policy)
- IU International University (private university)

The University of Potsdam offers an internationally recognized Toxicology program located within the Berlin–Brandenburg life science ecosystem.

The curriculum integrates pharmacology, toxicology, molecular biology, environmental sciences, and risk assessment.
